These teak logs undergo an even square cut and later processed in a multi rip saw. The next step is to reduce moisture by kiln drying. The de-moisturized lumbers go through moulding and double-end procedures.

The final products can be differentiated into TNG (Solid, Finger Joint), Rail, Panel Tabletop, Finger Joint, Skirting and Cornice.
