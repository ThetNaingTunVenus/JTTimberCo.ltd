Burma Teak Veneer

Since 1985

Jewellery Teak Timber Co., Ltd. obtains sustainable teak log by undergoing an auction process held by Myanma Timber Enterprise (MTE). Afterwards, we select and divide obtainable veneer quality teak logs.

Previous Next
Selected veneer logs are initially sawed in order to obtain Veneer Quarter and Crown Cut flitches and submerged in boiling water. Approximately after a week, the boiled veneer flitches are sliced into thin sheets. These thin veneer sheets are cut into specific sizes and dried to evaporate moisture.

Finally, we obtain our quality veneer products. It can be used for cabinet, furniture and decoration on Luxury Boats.
